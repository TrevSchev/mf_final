Little Miss Sunshine

Little Miss Sunshine is about a family going through some hard times. They all have their own problems and they all feel like they have to deal with them on their own. I have seen this movie multiple times and never really gave it too much thought beyond the fact that it is a fun comedy about a family where literally nothing can go right, but they deal with everything that is thrown at them just so they can get the youngest of their family, Olive, to the Little Miss Sunshine beauty pangent.

On my most recent viewing I realised that so much more is going on than just a family trying to do this thing for this little girl. What's really going on here is that Olive is the only sunshine in their lives. These adults and even the teenage son are already dealing with life and have lost their sunshine. They are all depressed in their own way, have their own issues, and are dealing with it however they can. Drugs, depression, shutting people out, and denial, but here they are setting all that aside for Olive the title "Little Miss Sunshine" simply refers to Olive and the sunshine she brings to her family.

Mad Max: Fury Road

Nux is a war boy and it is his and all war boy's desire to die for their overlord Immortan Joe and achieve their place in Valhalla. Early in the film a war boy yells "witness me" and in return the war boys witness him as he sacrifices himself to go to Valhalla. Shortly after Nux is about to pave his own path and with Max being the only one their he yells at Max to witness this moment, but Max won't let him. Nux fails in this moment and later on he fails Immortan Joe again. It is now clear to him that he is nothing to Immortan Joe, but Immortan Joe's "wives" show Nux compasion and kindness and he is able to assist in there escape. He contributes multiple skills and finds he is far more valuable than he ever thought he was. In the end Nux still sacrifices himself, but he does it out of love. Not obsession. To mirror the time he said it to Max at the beginning of the film instead of yelling he whispers the words witness me to the wives and they watch as he completes his journey to Valhalla.
