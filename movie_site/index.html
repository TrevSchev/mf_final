Let's talk About Movies

Let's share the insights that we have had with the movies that we have seen countless times. Movies can be enjoyed as fun thrill rides that take you to new places, but if we look a little deeper we can see that movies have the potential be masterfully crafted just like any great work of art.

Feel free to comment on the observations you have made about your favorite films. Here we want to know about anything you notice. Tell us about the theme, the characters, their arcs, the music, color, or even the lighting. Help us see Beyond the Film Reel.
