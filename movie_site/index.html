Through the Film

Let's talk About Movies

This site is a place where people can talk about their favorite movies. It is intended to be an open and free space where people can comment on the insight that they have had with the movies that they have seen countless times. Movies can be enjoyed as fun thrill rides that take you to new places, but if you look a little deeper than the surface you can see that movies can be masterfully crafted in their themes, characters, music, and even their colors just like a Monet or Picaso.
